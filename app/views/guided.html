Words

We look first at all words separately and polish them. Later, we rebuild all meaningful multiword tags with the polished words.

By default no words will be filtered. The removal of words is necessary if your dataset is big and uses a lot of computation time.

You might wanna start with a small dataset to find good parameters - and run the workflow afterwards with all words.

Blacklist

By default English stopwords and single characters will be removed. This reduces computation time and might improves the detection of meaningful single word tags.

The removal of stopwords reduces the quality of some multiword tags (Rock and Roll) - on the other hand keeping them creates nonsense groups like "of the"

Dictionary

By default the General Service List will be imported.

In small datasets in particular, you might want to import your own list, e.g. genres, to improve the quality of the spell correction algorithm.

Spell Correction

The algorithm searches for similar words and replaces the lower quality one by the higher quality one. You can choose which words shouldn't be changed and how similar words should be.

By default, words with a word quality above 0.7 will be treated as correct and words with a similarity above 0.7 will be replaced by the word with the higher quality.

Frequent Multiword Tags - Default

By default a group strength of 0.35 is used.

1 Means this group appeared most often and 0 it appeared never.

Unique Multiword Tags - Default

By default a group strength of 0.7 will be used.

1 Means this two words occur very often together an are less separated and 0 means the words are never together and often separated

Final Filtering

Select a set of important tags.

Only the select tags remain in the dataset.

The following step searches in the tags below the selected threshold for the words above or equal the threshold.

Salvager

You can change or remove tags from the selected tags. You might want to remove non-informative words or fix synonyms by hand.

Afterwards the salvager searches for the updated important tags in the not important tags. This increases the total dataset coverage.
